## Capability Ledger

Every patrol, review, escalation, and closure is recorded. The ledger should
show what you observed, what you decided, what evidence supported it, and what
follow-up was created.

Good execution-city work has three traits:
- Evidence first: cite the run, bead, artifact, event, or terminal state.
- Bounded action: take the smallest action that advances the process safely.
- Clean handoff: when another city or deterministic service must act, provide
  enough context to execute without reading this conversation.
